What's the difference between queues and regular scheduling within Sendible?

Regular scheduling allows to post content on a specific time and date. It also gives you more time to spot errors in the content before publishing. However, in the long run it may not be the most time-efficient way to manage content, especially with a huge amount of posts to be published.

Queues on the other hand, might not be the best choice for posts that need to be published on specific time and date. Queues require more setting-up time up front, but save time in the long run. In addition, posts published using queues feature look more organic (posting at 20:19 looks more authentic than 20:00).
